Bible Quiz Instructions

How to Play

Tap the screen or click your mouse to select the correct answers.

  • Quiz Controls
    • Click the play button on the welcome screen to begin the quiz.
    • There is a home button in the upper left corner and sound control in the upper right corner. The current question number is shown at the top of the quiz.
    • Click on the answer you believe to be correct for each of the 100 questions.
